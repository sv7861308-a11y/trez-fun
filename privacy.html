Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Welcome to ColoringJoy. Your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to being transparent about how we gather, utilize, and safeguard your information. This policy is written in plain language to help you understand our practices. Our aim is to collect only the data essential to provide and enhance our coloring pages and community features.

1. Information We Gather

To run our services, we gather a minimal amount of information. Here’s what we gather and why:

  • User Profile (Optional): If you create an account, we may gather a username, email address, and password. We use this to preserve your progress and secure your account. You are not required to create an account to use our coloring pages.
  • Usage Data: We automatically gather data about your activity, such as your saved art, color choices, and session length. This helps us improve our tools, fix bugs, and power features like the Artist Gallery.
  • User-Generated Content: We gather information you voluntarily provide, such as masterpieces you save or messages you send. Please be mindful not to share personal information in public spaces.
  • Contact Information: If you send us a message directly via our contact form, we will have a record of our correspondence, including your name and email address, to provide you with support.
  • Technical & Usage Data: We gather anonymous or aggregated data about how you interact with our studio, such as your browser type, device type, and time spent. We use this information to understand traffic, improve performance, and ensure compatibility.

2. How We Employ Your Information

Our primary goal is to provide a thrilling and safe questing environment. We employ your information to:

  • Operate and maintain our coloring studio.
  • Enable creative features, such as saving your work.
  • Display the Artist Gallery and personal art collections.
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., by showing your username).
  • Communicate with you for support or to respond to feedback.
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve our tools and develop new coloring pages.
  • Enforce our safety policies to ensure a respectful environment for all young artists.

3. Sharing Data and Other Guilds

We do not trade your personal information. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information if you give us explicit permission to do so.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services for hosting or analytics. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated to protect it.

4. Your Rights and Decrees

You hold dominion over your personal information. You have the right to:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your user profile through your settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear locally stored data, such as saved art, by clearing your browser's cache for our site.
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can unsubscribe from any newsletters we might send in the future.

5. Data Wards

We employ reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet-based service is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute safety.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly gather personal information from children under the age of 13 without parent/guardian oversight. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such info, we will erase it promptly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any queries or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@coloringjoy.com. We are here to help.
